About JP Technologies

Building AI-powered products end-to-end since 2023

Who We Are

JP Technologies is an independent software studio building AI-powered products that solve real problems. Founded by Joerg Peetz, we operate from Ireland and work globally, creating everything from life coaching applications to racing intelligence platforms to distributed agent orchestration systems.

Our Philosophy

We believe in end-to-end ownership. Every product we build is designed, developed, deployed, and maintained by us. This means:

  • Full responsibility for quality and reliability
  • Direct relationship with users
  • Complete control over our technology stack
  • Rapid iteration and improvement
  • No middlemen or unnecessary complexity

What We Build

Our product portfolio spans multiple domains:

  • BetterLife — Cognitive enhancement and life coaching for high performers
  • RaceIntel — AI-powered horse racing analysis and predictions
  • AgentForge — Distributed agent orchestration platform with multi-provider LLM routing
  • Helix — AI research engine combining meta-search with reasoning
  • Super Human Sol — Trading intelligence for Solana/Jupiter markets
  • BiteSaver — AI-powered recipe vault and meal planning (coming soon)

Our Infrastructure

Behind every product is a robust knowledge and infrastructure ecosystem:

  • Memex Zero-RAG — Compiled knowledge wiki with zero-hallucination lookups
  • Obsidian Mind Vault — Persistent memory and decision tracking
  • Code-Flow — Development workflow orchestration
  • Hermes Studio — Agent development environment
  • OpenClaw — Multi-channel agent control system (Telegram, Discord, Web)

Technology Stack

We leverage modern technologies including:

  • Frontend: Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, SwiftUI
  • Backend: Go, Python, Node.js, Vercel Functions, Docker
  • AI/ML: Claude API, OpenRouter, LangChain, Model Context Protocol (MCP)
  • Data: Supabase (PostgreSQL), Stripe, Redis
  • Infrastructure: Vercel, Contabo VPS, Docker containers

Our Values

  • Quality over quantity — We prioritize sustainable, well-built products
  • Privacy first — User data is treated as sacred
  • Transparency — We're clear about what our products do and their limitations
  • Continuous learning — We iterate, experiment, and improve constantly
  • User-centric design — Products are built to solve real problems

Location & Availability

We're based in Ireland but work with users and partners globally. We operate in multiple time zones and are available for collaboration, partnerships, and integration opportunities.
